"If a tree falls in a forest and no one is around to hear it, does it make a sound?"

Hello? What! You're not suppose to know about this place. How did you even get here? This website isn't even hosted anywhere. Nevermind that, since you are here you might as well read these. I like to write about stuff. Nothing weird. Usually just things that I have personal experiance with, such as dual-booting, golang, ipfs, vim, and more. As you can tell I'm a bit of a computer nerd, but don't worry, anyone should be able to follow along. If a person has something to say, and no one can understand, did they really say anything? Some of my posts are going to be tutorial-like where I describe steps to complete some task, but other posts will be about certain topics that I want to talk about such as the importance of simplicity.

Oh! One more thing before you go. While yes, technically other people can come here and read what I write, but I'm not neccessarly writing for a specific audience. Most of the time I write as if I am talking to myself, and for the most part I'm just doing this for myself. So if you don't like what I have to say, I probably don't care. However, if you would like to chat about whatever feel free to send mail to tylerbeverley@gmail.com.
